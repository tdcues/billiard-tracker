Run-Out Drill System (RDS) Summary

Always take ball in hand immediately after the break, scratch on break is allowed

Start with level 6 if this is your first time

You get additional BiH (ball in hand) on levels 2 - 9

Advance to next level if you win 2 out of 3 racks

Drop to previous level if you lose 3 in a row

Otherwise continue to shoot at current level
